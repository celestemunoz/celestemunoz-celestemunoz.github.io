About

As a first generation American, Celeste Munoz grew up in the heart of Los Angeles among the hustle and bustle of city life. The first of her family to graduate high school, Celeste sought opportunities to expand her education whilst providing for her parents and younger siblings. Celeste’s decision to join the United States Marine Corps brought about experiences she had never dreamed. Serving as an electronic maintenance repair technician, her attention to detail and proficiency were second to none. She rose through ranks and relished in the responsibility placed upon her shoulders. As a young adult, Celeste was exposed to true and natural beauty such as that in Fiji, and also to the raw and devastated vulnerability of places such as war-torn towns in Afghanistan. From her time in the Marine Corps, Celeste acquired a maturity, determination, and pure motivation that were to provide the foundation for her success in the future.

After eight years of experience with the Marines, Celeste chose to exit the military and continue her education by returning to school. Throughout her time with the military, Celeste developed a keen eye for finding beauty in all places and situations. This, along with her upbringing near the heart of the film industry, uncovered her passion for film editing.

Celeste is a student at Howard Community College whose studies have transcended the normal bounds of film and storytelling. Not one to follow a typical path, she has taken classes in all facets of entertainment, but has excelled in editing. With a soul that seems to belong in past eras, Celeste’s modest and simplistic nature shines through her work. Her ability to connect with all audiences appears effortless. Celeste’s devotion and maturity acquired through the Marine Corps are evident in her dedication to all tasks. Never shying away from a project, Celeste has experimented multiple different techniques and styles in many diverse genres. She is constantly striving for betterment and takes great care and satisfaction to ensure that her projects not only exceed expectations, but enhance each and every narrative, encapsulating the beauty of visual storytelling.
